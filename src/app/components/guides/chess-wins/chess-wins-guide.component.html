Chess Wins Guide

How to access Ye Buxiu, fulfill all prerequisites, and use Xiangqi.com to guarantee 3 chess wins per day for 900 Commerce Coins.

1. Introduction

Ye Buxiu is a chess NPC inside the Dreamscape who offers 3 wins at chess matches per day. By winning all 3 matches on Expert difficulty, you earn 300 Commerce Coins per win, totaling:

  • Daily total: 900 Commerce Coins
  • Weekly total: 6,300 Commerce Coins

However, losing a match costs you 150 Commerce Coins. To guarantee wins every time, we use Xiangqi.com as a simulated AI opponent.

How the Xiangqi.com Method Works

The website replicates the chess match externally:

  • "Hu" (the website AI) = represents your moves in-game
  • "Player" on the website = represents Ye Buxiu's AI moves

You mirror moves back and forth between the game and the website until the AI secures victory for you.

Quick Access: Use the embedded Chinese Chess tool in WWM Helper for convenient access to Xiangqi.com.

2. Requirements to Access Dreamscape

Before you can challenge Ye Buxiu, you must complete the following prerequisites:

  1. Defeat World Boss Yi Dao – Found in Qinghe region.
  2. Obtain 3 Chess Manuals – Defeat 3 chess NPCs scattered throughout the world.

Only after completing both requirements can you enter the Dreamscape via Yi Dao's sword and challenge Ye Buxiu at chess.

5. Using Xiangqi.com for Guaranteed Wins

This section explains how to set up the external Xiangqi.com tool to simulate and win your matches.

WWM Helper Integration: Open Tools → Chinese Chess to access Xiangqi.com inside a convenient frame.

5.1 Go to Play Computer

Configure the following settings:

  • Select: Play Computer
  • AI: Choose "Hu"
  • Side: Black
  • Position: Select "Custom Position"

5.2 Configure Board Editor Settings

Click Settings and switch the piece skin to one that closely matches the in-game icons. This makes it much easier to replicate moves accurately between the game and the website.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Restarting a match? Use Custom Position on Xiangqi.com to recreate the current board state and continue from there.
  • Misreplicated a move? Reset the board on the website to the last correct position and redo from that point.
  • Piece icons don't match? Go to Settings and try different piece skins until you find one that closely matches the in-game appearance.
