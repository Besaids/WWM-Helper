Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 3, 2025

Overview

WWM Helper is an unofficial companion tool for the game "Where Winds Meet". This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website.

WWM Helper does not run its own backend servers. All app logic runs in your browser, and most data stays on your device unless you choose to enable analytics.

Information We Collect

Local Storage

We store your preferences, checklist progress, and timer settings locally in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server controlled by WWM Helper.

Analytics (Optional)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This helps us improve the tool. When enabled, Google Analytics may collect:

  • Pages you visit
  • Time spent on the site
  • Browser type and device information
  • Approximate location (based on technical data)
  • Referring website

We do not ask you for or store your name, email, user accounts, or similar identifiers. When analytics is enabled, Google Analytics may process your IP address and other technical data for aggregation and geolocation, but this information is not visible to WWM Helper in reports and is not used to identify you personally.

Cookies and Local Storage

If you consent to analytics, Google Analytics uses cookies to track your session and measure site usage. You can reject analytics cookies at any time via the cookie banner (shown on your first visit, or again if you clear this site's data).

We also store a small consent flag in your browser (using local storage) to remember your choice about analytics. This preference never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server controlled by WWM Helper.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of analytics by rejecting cookies in the cookie banner
  • Clear your data by clearing this site's local storage and cookies in your browser
  • Change your mind about analytics at any time (you can clear this site's data in your browser settings to see the cookie banner again and choose a different option)

Data Retention

All data stored locally in your browser (preferences, checklists, timers, consent flag) persists until you manually clear it via your browser settings.

Analytics data is retained by Google according to our Google Analytics configuration and Google's own retention policies. For more details, see Google's documentation and privacy policy.
